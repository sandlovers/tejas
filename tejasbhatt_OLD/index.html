During Oktoberfest my favorite beer tent is the “Hacker-Zelt” associated with the brewery “Hacker Pschorr”. They also call it “Hack Pschorr Himmel der Bayern” (or the heaven of Bavarians).

Why do I like it so much? The roof is decorated with a clear blue sky dotted with small white clouds. The walls are painted with Bavarian villages.
